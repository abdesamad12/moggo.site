Account & Data Deletion

Email Login: mailbox app does not create or manage user accounts. All logins are handled securely by Yahoo, Outlook, or AOL. We do not store, collect, or have access to your personal data on our servers.

Data we delete: locally stored cache, cookies, and app preferences (removed automatically when you uninstall the app).

Data we do not control: your Yahoo/Outlook/AOL accounts, emails, or contacts. Please contact those providers directly to delete or manage your accounts with them.

If you would like to request deletion of app-related data or have concerns, please email us at:

support@moggo.site
